# **App Name**: Almustasharai

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure sign-up and login functionality for users, allowing access to personalized features.
- Consultant Profiles & Search: Users can browse and search for specialized legal consultants, view detailed profiles, and expertise areas.
- Consultation Booking System: A flexible system for users to schedule and book online legal consultation appointments with chosen consultants.
- Online Consultation Interface: A dedicated platform for users and consultants to conduct secure and efficient online legal consultations.
- Personal User Dashboard: A centralized dashboard for users to manage their personal information, view past consultations, and upcoming bookings.
- AI-powered Consultant Matching: An AI tool that suggests optimal legal consultants to users based on an analysis of their consultation request details and stated needs.
- Informational Pages: Access to static pages such as 'About Us', 'Privacy Policy', and 'Terms and Conditions' relevant to the platform's services.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional blue (#24488F), signifying trust and authority, ideal for a legal platform.
- Background color: A subtly desaturated, light blue-grey (#E7E9EF), providing a clean and understated canvas for content.
- Accent color: A vibrant cyan-blue (#3CC3DD), drawing attention to key interactive elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, objective, and highly legible characteristics, ensuring clarity in legal content.
- Employ a consistent set of clean, professional, line-based icons related to legal concepts, communication, and scheduling to enhance usability without distraction.
- Implement a clear, intuitive, and responsive layout, prioritizing mobile access for seamless online consultation experiences, with prominent navigation and well-organized content blocks.
- Incorporate subtle and functional animations for user feedback on interactive elements like form submissions, button clicks, and loading states, contributing to a polished user experience without being distracting.
